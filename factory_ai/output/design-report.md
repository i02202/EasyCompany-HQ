## DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

This visual mapping ensures:
1. **Consistent Pixel Density**: All tiles from Pixel Salvaje maintain uniform 32x32 base resolution
2. **Isometric Perspective**: Proper 2:1 pixel ratio for all furniture and props
3. **Color Harmony**: Zone-specific palettes that match floor tile specifications

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Pixel Density Standards
- All tiles: 32x32 base resolution
- Isometric ratio: 2:1 (width:height in screen space)
- Grid alignment: Props snap to 0.5-tile increments

### Layer Assignments
- **Below layer**: Desks, tables, large furniture (walkable behind)
- **Above layer**: Chairs, small props, people (render on top)

### Color Consistency Rules
1. **Cool zones** (Data Center, NOC): Grays, blues, blacks
2. **Warm zones** (CEO, CafĂ©, Architect): Woods, browns, burgundy
3. **Neutral zones** (Cowork, Huddle): Grays with teal accents
4. **Outdoor zones** (Terrace, Green): Natural greens and stone

### Fallback Geometric Styles
When tiles are unavailable, use solid color rectangles:
- **Desks**: #8B7355 (brown)
- **Chairs**: #4A4A4A (gray)
- **Screens**: #1A1A1A (black)
- **Plants**: #4A7C59 (green)
